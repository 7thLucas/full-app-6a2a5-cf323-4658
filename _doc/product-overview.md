# SusieQ Kitchen — Product Overview

## What it is
A **single-section, minimal landing page** for **SusieQ Kitchen**, a food/kitchen brand.
The page is deliberately one screen: a hero that introduces the brand and drives a single
clear action (order / contact / inquire). Priority is **speed** — it must load fast and feel
instant — and **restraint** — no extra sections, no clutter.

## Core promise
Turn a curious visitor into a customer on one screen. Brand name, one-line promise, one call
to action. Nothing competes for attention.

## Audience
- **Primary:** local customers discovering SusieQ Kitchen (via link, social bio, or search)
  who want to order food or reach the kitchen quickly.
- **Owner:** SusieQ Kitchen, who needs a fast, presentable web presence without a multi-page site.

## Scope (v1)
- Exactly **one section** (hero): brand name/logo, headline promise, short supporting line,
  one primary CTA, minimal contact/order affordance.
- **No** secondary sections (no menu grid, about, gallery, footer-heavy layout) in v1.

## Brand & tone
- **Palette:** pink / reddish — warm, appetizing, inviting (primary rose-red, soft pink accents),
  on a clean light background.
- **Tone:** warm, simple, confident. Homey but tidy.

## Strategic principles
- **Minimal by design** — one section, one job. Resist scope creep.
- **Fast first** — lightweight, instant load is a feature, not a nicety.
- **One clear action** — a single CTA, no competing links.

## Open assumptions (to confirm during onboarding)
- Exact offering of SusieQ Kitchen (restaurant / bakery / home kitchen / catering) — **assumed: prepared food**.
- The single primary action — **assumed: "Order now" / "Contact"**.
- Order volume / pricing — unknown; North Star is projected from labeled assumptions.
