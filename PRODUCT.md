# SusieQ Kitchen — Landing Page
- A single-section, very minimal landing page for SusieQ Kitchen.
- Users: local customers/visitors discovering SusieQ Kitchen online, deciding whether to order or reach out.
- The one job: ONE beautiful hero section that converts a visitor into an order/inquiry.
- Content: brand name "SusieQ Kitchen", a warm mouth-watering tagline, one primary CTA (Order / Contact), optional small supporting line.
- Tone: warm, appetizing, confident, simple.
- Scope: ONE section only. No navigation, no extra pages, no clutter. Speed and simplicity above all.
